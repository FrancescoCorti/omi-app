About the data

Prices are sourced from the OMI (Osservatorio del Mercato Immobiliare), the official Italian real estate observatory managed by the Italian Taxing Agency (Agenzia delle Entrate). Data covers residential and commercial property types across all Italian territories, updated every semester. Learn more →
