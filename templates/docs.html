User Guide

Everything you need to navigate the data, interpret the charts, and make the most of the interactive map.

01 — Chart Guide

This page will guide you through the functionalities of the Chart page.

Buy Price Trends chart

Select a location

Use the dropdown menu on top of the chart to select a location and view its price trends. Open the dropdown menu and write the name a location to find it faster.

The selection menu follows a cascade logic to filter locations.

Region → Province → Municipality → Zone

Notice that the chart will display the average values whenever no further areas are selected.

Select property type and condition

The selection of specific property types and conditions is available at every moment, regardless of the selected location. "Residential housing" is displayed as the default type, while conditions are averaged unless specifically selected.

Select a specific time range

To plot a specific time range, use the TIME RANGE button. By default, the maximum time range is displayed, while 5 and 10 years are available as alternative options.

Notice that the time range selection is limited by data availability. For instance, with a zone selected, the maximum time range extends to 2014.

Visualize the number of transactions

To integrate the chart with information relative to transaction volumes, use the TRANSACTIONS button. Click on one of the available options to visualize the yearly number of transactions for the selected location. Click again on the button to clear the previous selection or click on another option to switch.

Notice that the selection is available only when one of the residential properties (Residential housing, Lowcost housing, Independent houses and villas, Luxury housing) is selected as property type. Transaction records are available only from 2014.

% Change chart

Scroll down in the chart page and discover the % Change graph where you can see periodic changes of real estate prices.

Use the CHANGE PERIOD button to switch between semestral or 12-month percentage price change.

The interactive banners in the Buy Price Trend chart and in the % Change graph are synchronized. Hover with the cursor to simultaneously display data on both graphs.

02 — Map Guide

This page will guide you through the functionalities of the Map page.

National selection

Select the National option in the SCOPE button to display buy prices for regions or provinces, relative to the latest available semester.

Custom selection

Select the Custom option in the SCOPE button to customize the research to a specific region or provinces.

Region level

With the Region option selected in the LEVEL button, a region must be chosen from the dropdown menu. With a Region selected, proceed by choosing specific provinces or municipalities in the relative dropdwon menus (optional).

Prefer this option when the focus is on a specific region rather than on interregional areas.

Province level

With the Province option selected in the LEVEL button, a maximum of 5 provinces can be selected from the dropdown menu. With provinces selected, proceed by choosing specific municipalities in the dropdown menu.

Prefer this option when the focus is on selected areas across multiple regions.

Perimeters selection

Use the PERIMETERS button to choose how to render perimeters (municipality or zone) on the map. The button is always active upon satisfaction of the location requirements.
